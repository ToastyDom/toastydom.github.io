We are happy to present the Brain Encoding Response Generator (BERG) at Cognitive Computational Neuroscience 2026 as part of our Keynote & Tutorial, **“In silico neuroscience: an emerging paradigm for brain discovery.”**

During the session, we will introduce BERG as a toolbox for generating in silico neural responses using pretrained brain encoding models, and provide a hands-on tutorial on how it can be used for large-scale experiments in computational neuroscience.

More information about the event can be found here:  
https://2026.ccneuro.org/k-and-t-in-silico/
